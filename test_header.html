First Header: Uses width/height expansion for the hover effect. Hover over it to see the lime green background sweep from bottom-left to top-right, while the stripes change color.

Second Header: Uses clip-path for a more precise diagonal sweep animation. This creates a cleaner diagonal transition effect.

Both animations have a 1.5s duration and reverse when you stop hovering. The stripes are slanted using CSS transform skewX(-20deg) and positioned on the right side.
